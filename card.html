Hummingbird

Hummingbirds eat more than their body weight every day.

  • Scientific Name: Trochilidae
  • Average Length: 7.5 - 13 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: North and South America

Though small in size, hummingbirds are hard to miss. The males have bright, irridescent plumage, and in flight the birds' rapid flapping creates a clear buzzing sound.
